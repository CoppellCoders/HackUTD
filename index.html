What DormDash Features

Prepare to be Shook!

Availability

Our reliable, student runners (a.k.a Cometeers) will be able to accomodate your hungry needs at any time available! Whether that be breakfast from the Dining Hall or dinner from the Student Union, we have you covered at all times!

Meal Exchanges

DormDash offers Meal Exchanges! How exciting is that? And don't worry, we won't be needing your Comet Card! We ensure that the Cometeer uses only a secured, read-only version of your Comet Card barcode to get your meal exchange for whatever place you desire!

Easy Pickup!

The Cometeer makes pickup the most convienient for you. If you live in a Resident Hall, then no need to walk further than neccesary. The Cometeer will come up to lobby area of your floor and pick it up from them. If you live in University Village, Canyon Creek Heights, or Northside, then we can deliver directly right to your door without any problems!

Hiring

Join The Cometeer Team! We are always looking for more potential Cometeers to join our service. If you are looking for a job with flexible hours, then this could be the job for you! A Cometeer only works when their schedule fits the job! So, why wait? Start applying now!
